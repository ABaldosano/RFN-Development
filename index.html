About the Game

A Life Simulation
Built Around Real Progression

Rise From Nothing is an incremental life simulation. You start at the very bottom with no money and no options, then work your way up through careers, business ownership, and financial choices that actually matter.

It works for people who want to grind for an hour straight and for people who check in twice a day to collect earnings. Free to play, with progress driven by the decisions you make in the game.

What's Inside

Core Gameplay Systems

Career Progression
You start as a beggar. Each job earns more than the last, and each one opens up whatever comes next. There is no skipping ahead.
Business Ownership
Buy a business, hire workers, and let income come in without doing the active work yourself. Upgrade it when you have the capital.
Passive Economy
Income runs even when you close the game. When you come back, the earnings are waiting. No need to stay glued to the screen.
Vehicles & Property
Cars and real estate are progression markers, not decorations. Owning them unlocks new parts of the game you cannot reach otherwise.
Reputation System
How people see you changes as your wealth grows. A higher reputation opens doors that money alone cannot buy.
Cross-Platform Play
Play in a browser on your computer or phone, or install the Android app. Your save carries over between all of them.
